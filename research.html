Research Interests

My research focuses on the intersection of statistics, machine learning, and computational methods to solve complex real-world problems, with an emphasis on responsible AI, algorithmic fairness, and data-driven solutions for healthcare, energy, climate, and public health.

Key Research Areas:

  • Computational Statistics
  • Responsible AI and Algorithmic Fairness
  • Energy Economics Modeling
  • Healthcare Data Analytics
  • Climate Science Applications
  • Probabilistic Graphical Models
  • Statistical Data Mining
