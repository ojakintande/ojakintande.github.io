About Me

Dr. Olalekan Joseph Akintande is a statistician and researcher specializing in Machine Learning, Computational Statistics, and Statistical Learning. With a PhD in Computational Statistics from the University of Ibadan, Nigeria, he currently serves as a Postdoctoral Researcher at DTU Compute, focusing on responsible AI, algorithmic fairness, and data-driven solutions for healthcare and global challenges. Cited over 550 times, his work bridges interdisciplinary applications in energy economics, public health, climate science, and AI ethics.
