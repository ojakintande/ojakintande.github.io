About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Technical University of Denmark, specializing in econometrics, energy economics, and environmental policy. My research focuses on developing innovative quantitative methods to address pressing energy and environmental challenges.

With a PhD in Economics and over 5 years of research experience, I have published in leading journals and contributed to several international research projects. My work combines theoretical rigor with practical applications to inform policy decisions.
